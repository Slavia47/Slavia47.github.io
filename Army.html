Infantry

The role of infantry in modern combat comprize of being the primary "protector" in the combined arms model. This is due to their cheap equipment meaning the cost of lots of atrition can be absorbed. Additional therefor they are plentiful and compromise most of the military(normaly). Furthermore they are a necessity as they fufill multiple niches in the military that the others cant. The most crutial of these at the operational level is Urban warfare. This is due to the plentiful vertical elevation that can be found this makes it difficult for any other part of the military to fight in. for more information aboput urban warfare see Wiki's urban warfare or see YT Task & Pourpose Further uses include protecting valuable targets including everything from tanks, land and people. This consists of IFV, APC and AAV. Thease exist to help infantry in their job: providing moving cover; fire support; and AT capabilities normaly in the form of ATGMs. They also provide much needed mobility.
