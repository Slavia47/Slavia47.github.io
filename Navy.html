Submerines

convential submerines( torpedo tubes)

Have only two goals. Primarily sink enemy logistics convoys or sink any enemy warship that gets in its way.

missile submerines

Used to stealthily sneak up to coast and bombard costal instalations used to destroy ships in harbour or make the harbour unusable shrinking the enemys navl range. They can also be equiped with nukes and quickly launched in a first strike on a nation.

corvette

Primarily small ship for convoy escort and normaly equiped with anti submerine equipment such as depth charges.

destroyer

Is a fast, maneuverable, long-endurance warship intended to escort larger vessels in a fleet, convoy, or battle group and defend them against powerful short-range attackers

Cruiser

Ever since the retirment of the battleships they are the largest after the aircraft carrier. Therfore the heviest armed with multipe anti ship missiles missle and air defence and conventional guns.

Aircraft carrier

Used to ferry and control aircraft. They will deploy airwings with anti ship missiles and torpedos.
